Search Engine Optimization

Search engine optimization is abbreviated as SEO. Search engine optimization is a combination of two words, “search engine” and “optimization”. Search engine is a tool that displays the information according to the keywords whereas optimization is an act to make best of something.

It is the process by which the website rank can be optimized using different strategies. Simply, it is a set of rules to optimize the online content so that the search engine displays the website at the top of the results.

SEO helps the website to get accessed by the search engine. This process is followed to make sure that the content of the website is crawled by the search engines like Google,yahoo, Bing etc. and is indexed on the results. SEO mainly focuses on the organic/natural result rather than the paid promotions.

SEO helps to promote any business and attract more visitors towards the website. Also, SEO does not only mean ranking the website at the top but also understanding what people usually search online, most used keywords in order to search something and what content are they seeking for.

Therefore, the first strategy of search engine optimization is to understand the audience’s intent and then delivering it the way search engine can find them.
